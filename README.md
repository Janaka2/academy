Janaka Academy

Empowering a new generation of engineers to surpass technical mastery with spiritual depth and ethical integrity.

Vision

My mission is to awaken the giant within a thousand souls—guiding each to surpass me not just in engineering skill, but in spiritual depth and ethical integrity. Together, step by step, we’ll create a tribe of leaders who innovate with compassion, live by principle, and make the world kinder and easier for everyone.

What We Offer

Step‑by‑step learning paths: Comprehensive curriculum covering Java, Spring Boot, AI integration, and other technologies that enable you to build scalable, modern solutions.

Hands‑on projects: Real‑world exercises that teach you how to design, develop, and deploy enterprise systems.

Mentorship and community: Access to a supportive network of mentors and peers committed to your success.

Ethical and spiritual growth: Guidance on aligning your technical journey with strong ethics and a deeper sense of purpose.

Join the Mission

Everyone is welcome! Here’s how you can participate:

Star and watch this repository to stay informed about new learning materials, events, and updates.

Fork the repo and submit pull requests with improvements, study notes, or new content. Your contributions help shape the academy.

Join our community channels (Discord/Slack links will be provided) to connect with fellow learners, ask questions, and share insights.

Spread the word — invite others who are eager to grow technically, spiritually, and ethically. Together we aim to empower at least 1,000 people to exceed their own expectations and serve the world.
